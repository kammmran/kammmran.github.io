## About the guest

Dr. Valentina Erestova provides consultancy and collaborative R&D in molecular-level modelling to support materials design, formulation and process-relevant questions across energy, environment and advanced materials. Using atomistic simulation, HPC and data-driven workflows, she helps partners understand how molecules adsorb, move and interact in complex porous materials and at interfaces. Her main expertise is in clay minerals, biochar and other carbon materials, and organic-mineral interfaces.
