## AnimAI

**AnimAI** is a competition designed for prompt engineers, anime enthusiasts, and editors or designers. In this event, participants are provided with a 5-minute anime edit video. Following this, we give them four key prompts, such as *'Naruto,' 'cat,' 'forest,'* and *'cloud'.* Competitors have 90 minutes to create images using Canva (DALL-E) based on these prompts. Additionally, they are required to write a short story inspired by their generated image. 

### Submission Requirements:
- Prompts
- Final image
- Accompanying story text

A panel of mentors will evaluate each entry and assign points based on the quality of the prompts, the image, and the story. Competitors may join individually or in teams. Individual participants will be assigned to groups consisting of:
- One anime fan
- One prompt engineer
- One photo editor
