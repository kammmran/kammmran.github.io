## TruthRadar

This project was developed during a **2-hour vibe coding competition** organized by the **GDG Baku** community.

**TruthRadar** is a quiz-based platform where users try to determine whether news images and videos are **real, fake, or AI-generated**. The goal is to raise awareness about misinformation and help users improve their media literacy.

For development, I used **open datasets from Kaggle** to build the quiz content.

### Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js  
- **Backend:** Django
