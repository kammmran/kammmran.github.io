FreeBee is a travel assistance platform designed to simplify trip planning by providing essential information for both domestic and international travel. It helps users save time and money by offering detailed roadmaps, budget estimates, event calendars, and more.



## Project Overview

**Idea:**  
FreeBee aims to address the common challenges travelers face, such as high costs, time-consuming trip planning, and safety concerns. The platform provides curated travel information, including visa requirements, recommended travel seasons, budget guidance, local festivals, and more. By leveraging user feedback and expert insights, FreeBee continuously improves its offerings.



## Problems and Solutions

- **High Fees and Time Investment:**  
  Travelers often struggle with costly tours and lengthy trip planning. FreeBee offers free roadmaps, budget-friendly tour plans, and access to user feedback to save users both time and money.



- **Unexpected Situations:**  
  Situational changes during trips can create issues for travelers. FreeBee addresses this by gathering and incorporating real-time feedback from users to adapt and improve its plans.


- **Safety Concerns:**  
  Safety is a priority for travelers. FreeBee partners with tour leaders and local experts to provide safe travel experiences and stay in constant contact with users during their trips.

## Business Model

FreeBee operates on a subscription-based membership model, with three tiers:
- **Free:** Access to basic maps without detailed itineraries.
- **Gold:** Includes detailed roadmaps, discounted tours, free festival enrollments, and the ability to earn money by submitting feedback.
- **Platinum:** Access to all Gold benefits plus five free tours and complimentary tickets.

All plans are available as monthly or yearly subscriptions.



## Research Insights

FreeBee's roadmap data sources, such as tourismboard.az, provide comprehensive but complex maps. FreeBee simplifies these maps, making them easier for users to understand and use effectively.
