CHRONO SHIFT — COMBAT EDITION

A neon-soaked platformer where timing wins fights. Pick a mode, drop in, kill everything, and reach the portal at the far end of each level.

What's new in this build

  • No more auto-heal. Health does not regenerate on its own. Kill enemies and pick up the pink HEAL drops they leave behind to recover.
  • Heal on the map. Heal items also spawn among the random orbs scattered through each level — keep an eye out.
  • Skills reworked. Skills are now bound to Q E R in 1P mode so they no longer fight with jump or movement.
  • Time-slow removed. Holding Space no longer slows time; Space now jumps in 1P. Use B for Rewind.
  • Modes split. 1-Player and 2-Player live in their own pages with their own keybindings, so the two never conflict.
  • Settings. Pick a music track, toggle music, adjust music + SFX volume.

Goal

Survive, score, and reach the portal. On HARD/PRO the portal stays locked until you clear every enemy. PRO enemies shoot back.
