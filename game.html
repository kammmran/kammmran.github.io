CHRONO SHIFT

A neon-soaked vertical platformer where timing wins fights. Pick a mode, pick a map, pick a hero — then climb. Solo, local two-player split-screen, or online co-op via a 6-character room code.

🎯OBJECTIVE

Climb upward to the portal at the top of the map. HARD / PRO require all enemies dead first. PRO enemies shoot back. In ENDLESS mode there is no portal — keep climbing until you die.

🗺MAPS
  • NEON ASCENT — beginner climb, wide platforms.
  • CHRONO SPIRE — crumbling platforms required.
  • VOID FOUNDRY — lava floor, dense saws, big gaps. LIFTER recommended.
  • ENDLESS — procedural climb; difficulty ramps every 1000m.
✨WHAT'S NEW IN THIS BUILD
  • Home Base hub. Roster, gun store, coupon entry, achievements all in one full-screen page.
  • Gun Store. 5 new buyable guns (Shotgun, Sniper, Rail Gun, Flame, Rocket) with a 3-slot equip loadout.
  • Coupon system. Redeem codes for coins. Try kammmran.
  • Endless mode. No portal, no level break — climb until you die. Difficulty ramps per 1000m.
  • Achievements. 6 milestones tracking distance, kills, score, and level progress.
  • Classic gold coins. The coin pickup got a proper spinning-gold-coin look.
💾SAVE DATA

Everything persists in browser localStorage: coins, upgrades, owned guns, equipped loadout, achievements, redeemed coupons, best score. Clear it from Settings → Reset All Progress.

🩸HEALTH & DROPS

No auto-regen. Pink ❤ heal drops are scattered across the map and also drop from kills. Plasma + time-gun ammo also drops from kills.

🎯HOW TO WIN

Climb up to the portal at the TOP of the map. HARD / PRO require all enemies dead first; the portal stays locked otherwise. PRO enemies shoot back.

In ENDLESS mode there is no portal. The map regenerates as you reach the top and the difficulty ramps every 1000m. The run ends only when your HP hits 0.
